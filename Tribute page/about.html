Timeline and About of Heneral Antonio Luna

Heneral Antonio Luna's Timeline:

  • 1866 - Born in Binondo, Manila, Philippines
  • 1890 - Earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from Ateneo Municipal de Manila
  • 1894 - Completed his Doctorate in Pharmacy in Spain
  • 1898 - Appointed Chief of War Operations in the Philippine Revolutionary Army
  • 1899 - Led Filipino forces during the Battle of Calumpit against American troops
  • 1899 - Assassinated in Cabanatuan by soldiers under the command of his rivals

About Heneral Antonio Luna:

Heneral Antonio Luna was a prominent Filipino general during the Philippine-American War. Known for his fierce temperament and strategic military mind, he played a crucial role in the fight against American colonization. Luna was not only a military leader but also a skilled pharmacist and an advocate for the establishment of a national army. Despite his contributions, he faced internal conflicts and rivalries within the revolutionary ranks, which ultimately led to his assassination in 1899. His legacy as a national hero continues to inspire many Filipinos today.
